Project Characterization

ECA Points

Stations have been facing inventory management difficulties without a digital tool to track materials, and lacked a feasible way for citizens to reach them.

Problem #1

Citizens

Citizens had no access to recycling education, lacked visibility into ECA point materials and inventory capacity, and had no direct channels to contact station managers.

Problem #2
Moving on to the project characterization. When we were researching the inventory management difficulties that the ECA points had been facing, and the lack of a feasible way for citizens to communicate with them, we realized that we should develop a scalable solution. Therefore, we have built a platform that allows citizens to visualize educational information about recycling, and to have the option of being part of a community. On the other hand, we have integrated an interactive map with all registered ECA points where the user not only can see contact information, but also can visualize the material availability in real-time. Finally, we provided a direct chat system. If we had not built this platform, the communication between citizens and stations would have continued to be fragmented and inefficient.

Grammar: Past Continuous, Simple Past, Should, Present Perfect, Third Conditional

Target Market

Citizens of Bogotá

Common citizens interested in recycling who have been searching for accessible information on where and how to recycle. They will get used to having a platform with ECA point locations, materials, and real-time inventory.

ECA Station Operators

Station administrators who should supervise purchase and sale transactions of recyclable materials. They are used to managing inventory manually, but through our platform they would digitalize their operations.

Product / Service Identification

Three fundamental cores focused on managing recycling materials efficiently and creating an educational space.

Admin Panel

ECA administrators have been supervising purchase and sale transactions of recyclable materials through this panel.

Social Module

Citizens will be able to see content and acquire knowledge about collection, management, and recycling.

Direct Chat

A direct chat between citizen and collection point that ensures fluid communication without intermediaries between these two actors.

Through our web platform, we have offered a digital service that has been seeking to differentiate itself from a basic blog. First of all, we have disposed of an administrative panel. In the second place, there will be a social module for citizens. Finally, we have incorporated a direct communication system. If we had not built these three cores, communication would have been less effective.
